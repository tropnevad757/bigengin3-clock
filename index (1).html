SIGNALS TAB

4 Checks. One Decision.

01
ZONE ACTIVE
Z1, Z4, Z6, Z7, Z10, or Z12 must be live. No zone = no trade. The clock shows which zone is active in real time.
02
PRIME POCKET
Highest-probability sub-window inside the zone. White arc appears on clock when active. Wait for the pocket when possible.
03
DXY · CORRELATION ALIGNMENT
Set DXY direction — falling dollar = BTC bullish. Inverse instruments must confirm zone direction. One tap to cycle RISING / FALLING / NEUTRAL.
